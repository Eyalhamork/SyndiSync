# Veo 3 Video Generation Prompts

These prompts are optimized for Google's Veo 3 model in Vertex AI. They are designed to create a cohesive, cinematic "Fintech Premium" aesthetic.

**Style Guide Applied:** 
*   **Visual Tone:** Cinematic 4K, Photorealistic, Anamorphic lens, High contrast, Cool corporate colors (navy blue, silver, white) with warm gold accents.
*   **Lighting:** Volumetric office lighting, dramatic shadows, soft diffusions.
*   **Motion:** Smooth cinematic camera movements (slow dolly, truck, or rack focus).

**Option A: The Screen Glare (First Person)**
*Concept:* The immediate stress of seeing the inbox.
**Prompt:**
> Cinematic close-up shot of a computer monitor in a dimly lit modern office at night. The screen is filled with an endless scrolling list of unread emails with red notification badges. *Concept:* The feeling of being overwhelmed by noise.

**Option A: The Overflowing Desk (Reality)**

**Option C: The Loading Bar (Digital Frustration)**
*Concept:* Slow software.
**Prompt:**
> POV shot of a computer screen. A "Loading..." progress bar is stuck at 99%. The cursor spins endlessly. Screen pixels are visible. Reflection of a frustrated user in the screen. Tense, annoying, relatable delay.
